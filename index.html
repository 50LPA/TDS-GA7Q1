Financial Formula

Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC):

\\[ \mathrm{WACC} = \frac{E}{V} \cdot R_e \;+\; \frac{D}{V} \cdot R_d \cdot (1 - T) \\]

Explain variables: E=Equity, D=Debt, V=E+D, Re=Cost of Equity, Rd=Cost of Debt, T=Tax Rate.
